House Drinks

Available in a shot, a cocktail, or a tall glass.

Blue Otter Pop - Raspberry vodka, blue curacao, fresh lemon juice, simple syrup, sweet & sour, topped with Sprite.

Don’s Rye-Not Old Fashioned - Rebel Yell Rye whiskey, sugar, Angostura bitters with roasted orange and lemon slices.

Me-Mum’s Strawberry Lemonade - Citrus vodka, strawberry schnapps, simple syrup, fresh moen juice, lemonade, topped with Sprite.

Banana Nut Bread - RumChata, banana liqueur, a splash of club soda and Sprite.

Caribbean Screw - Coconut rum, peach schnapps, and banana liqueur topped with orange & pineapple juice, with a splash of milk.

Georgia Peach - Canadian whiskey, peach schnapps, and pineapple juice.

California Cooler - J.Riley California Clear whiskey, peach schnapps, and a splash of orange juice dropped in energy drink.

Irish Dr. Pepper - Irish whiskey, butterscotch schnapps, Irish cream and Dr. Pepper topped with whipped cream.

Sucker Punch - A shaken blend of Cruzan 151 rum, peach schnapps, melon liqueur, banana liqueur, and butterscotch schnapps with orange and pineapple juice.

Unicorn Blood - Pear cider poured over ice with a float of red wine.

Molotov Cocktail - Irish whiskey and Irish cream, with a splash of butterscotch schnapps.

Blueberry Warhead - Blueberry vodka, lime juice, and blue curacao.

Fruit Loop - Orange, blueberry, and raspberry vodkas with a blend or orange, pineapple, and cranberry juice, topped with Sprite and a splash of peach schnapps.

Candy Apple - Watermelon and sour apple schnapps, raspberry vodka, topped with energy drink or sprite.

Action Jackson - Coconut rum, citrus vodka, and melon liqueur, with a mix of pineapple and cranberry juice.

Riptide Rush - Citrus vodka and grape pucker topped with sweet & sour and club soda.

Abuelita - A blend of RumChata, Patron XO, whipped cream and cold brew coffee.

Ol-Greg - An old fashioned Margarita on the rocks, with a kick of Tabasco and a Tajin rim.

Butterscotch Cookie - Coffee Liqueur, butterscotch schnapps, Irish cream and cointreau.

Cranberry Kamikaze - Vodka, triple sec, lime juice, simple syrup, a squeeze of fresh lime and a splash of cranberry juice.

Spritzer Twister - Vodka, sprite, lime juice and a float of red wine.

The Garcia - Jameson Irish whisky with Coke and a splash of pineapple.

T’s Punch - A delicious, layered treat: melon liqueur, rum, coconut rum and vodka with a blend of orange & pineapple juices, topped with cranberry juice.
