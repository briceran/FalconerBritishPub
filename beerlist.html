  • Spritzer Twister : Vodka, sprite, lime juice and a float of red wine.
  • The Garcia : Jameson Irish whisky with Coke and a splash of pineapple.
  • T’s Punch : A delicious, layered treat: melon liqueur, rum, coconut rum and vodka with a blend of orange & pineapple juices, topped with cranberry juice.
